## The Challenge

In an era where creators are increasingly seeking sustainable income streams beyond traditional employment, existing platforms take 20-30% of creator revenue. Creators deserve better.

## The Solution

HIVE Club is a next-generation creator subscription platform designed to transform how content creators monetize their work and connect with their communities.

### Industry-Leading Revenue Split

Unlike established platforms, HIVE Club offers an unprecedented 90/10 revenue split, allowing creators to keep 90% of every subscription dollar earned.

### Premium Features for Serious Creators

- **Three-tier subscription system** (Bronze, Silver, Gold Hive) for flexible monetization
- **Customizable HIVE Link** bio pages for seamless fan conversion
- **Direct messaging system** for authentic community building
- **Built-in content management** with support for images, videos, and text
- **Real-time analytics** and subscriber insights
- **Integrated payment processing** via Stripe

## The Philosophy

"This isn't just about making money. It's about giving creators the tools to build sustainable businesses while maintaining direct relationships with their supporters. No algorithms. No middlemen. Just creators and the people who value their work."

## Launch

HIVE Club launches January 1, 2026 and is now accepting creator applications at [hiveclub.co](https://hiveclub.co).
